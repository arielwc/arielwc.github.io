  • What is your long term goal and or career pathway?

  • What skills (non-technical - human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at EDA?

    Time-management, energy management, focus management. Update: Since reading more about emotional intelligence, this might be a thing too.

  • A description of your own strengths and limitations

    My strengths lie in analysis, problem-solving, articulating and processing. I can think about the big picture and get lost in fine details, doing these things simultaneously can be difficult though.

    My limitations would definitely include time-management and organisation. I tend to underestimate how long I’m going to need to do something. I tend to jump to saying “I can do this in 20 minutes”, whereas I should be saying “I could push myself to do this in 20 minutes, assuming I was fully rested, fed and caffeinated, had no distractions and all processes involved went swimmingly every step of the way, but life happens and I don’t want to stress myself out so it’s going to take an hour.”
